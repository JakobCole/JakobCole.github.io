In my first podcast, I am joined by fellow group members of "Starving Artists" Josh Graham, Clare Carter, Trenton Spillman, and Amanda Holt to discuss the growing sentiment of franchise film fatigue and the lack of original movies in the film industry.

We discuss everything from the definition of franchise film fatigue (0:26), the public's obsession with remakes (1:36), some staggering movie statistics (3:00), our favorite original movies (5:05), the need for precedence in pitching movies to studios (7:55), the growing number of female leads in original movies (9:21), and close with thesis time (10:30).
